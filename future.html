The Future!

The future is the reason I do everything. I can't wait to go to college, meet new people, work, and discover the world.

I don't believe there's a 'purpose' for my life. I want to understand as much as I can, which is why I always ask 'why?'.

I think life is one paragraph, not a collection of chapters.

My current plan is to go to:

  • Double major in Computer Science and Business.
  • Take online courses oriented around data science.
  • Get as much experience in those fields through internships.
  • Get a masters in Computer Science.
  • Work at Tesla, Apple, Google, Microsoft or Nvidia.
  • Start my own project in the field I work in.
  • Generate loads of wealth and become the biggest philanthropist.

That's my life plan

I want to be 120, sitting outside in a rocking chair with my wife, around our great great grandkids answering their questions of what I did after school and be proud of the 100 years of work I put in.
